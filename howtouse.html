How to Use

StorySaver helps you save Video, Photo, Stories, Reels and Highlight Post from Instagram. Use StorySaver to download and keep your favorite Instagram content for offline viewing. The app supports all common post types — IGTV, Reels, Stories, Highlights, Photos, and Videos. Whether you want to save feed videos, IGTV, or a friend’s Stories and Highlights, StorySaver makes the process fast and straightforward.

Hello Welcome!

Welcome to StorySaver! Browse a wide range of photos, Videos and Stories from Instagram in a single place. To access private content (private profiles or private Stories), please log in with your Instagram credentials. After logging in you will be able to view and download content that is available to your account.

Home Screen!

The Home Screen is the main hub for the app. From here, you can paste copied links, start downloads, browse available Stories and posts, and manage bookmarks. Use the menu for quick access to settings, help, and other features to tailor the app to your needs.

  • Three buttons at the bottom let you switch between All Stories, Home, and the Instagram Website.
  • Access bookmarks, media history, and frequent profiles directly from the Home Screen.
  • If the "paste preview" setting is turned off, link previews will not be shown.
  • All frequently used actions are available in the menu for quick access.

Copy Paste Download
Open Instagram and tap the Share button to copy a post link.

When you choose Copy Link in Instagram's share sheet, the link is copied to your clipboard.

Open StorySaver — the app will detect the copied link and show a preview with the linked photos and videos. From that preview you can download the entire set or choose individual items.

After the preview of the pasted link appears, you have two options:

  • Download all items at once using the Download button next to Paste.
  • Download a single image or video by tapping the download option on the specific preview item.

Similarly, you can copy the URL of the:

  • Stories
  • reels
  • highlights
  • User Profile

If you prefer not to copy the link, you can also open the post in Instagram and use the Share option to send it directly to IsSave for download.

Login with Instagram

You must log in to download Instagram Stories and to download media from private accounts. Logging in enables access to content that is visible only to your account.

Or

To log in using Instagram, enter your Instagram username and password when prompted. You can then download content directly from the in-app browser or by using the copy-paste method.

After successful login, you may use the app in the same way as with public content — preview, select, and download.

How to Logout

To log out, open the app menu, select Logout, and confirm when the dialog appears.

How to Bookmark

To bookmark a profile, copy the user's profile URL or username. Then go to the Bookmark section and choose Add User to save the profile for quick access.

Use of StorySaver App

  • Stories

View Stories from users; the story count is shown so you can see how many are available.

If you encounter a "Could not refresh feed" message, this usually indicates a temporary issue with Instagram's service or session expiration. Try again later, and avoid logging into your account on untrusted third-party apps.

  • search users

Use the search feature (login required) to find Instagram users by username.

  • Profile

Open any user's profile to view available posts, Stories, and Highlights (subject to the profile's privacy settings).

  • Highlights Download

View and download a user's Highlights with a single tap when they are available.

  • Media Download options

Choose to download entire albums or single items depending on what you need. The app shows options to download all media at once or pick individual photos or Videos.

  • Saved Items and long press feature

Access your saved items quickly. Use long press on any saved item to reveal additional actions (share, delete, or open in app).

  • Download Items in Your Gallery

Downloaded Photos and Videos are saved to your device gallery for offline viewing.

  • Settings

Use Settings to enable or disable auto-download, clear download history, and manage app data and preferences.

  • Feedback

Share feedback or report issues directly with the developer team. For quick support, check "Need Quick Support" to contact via WhatsApp.

  • Hidden Feature Instructions

If you have read this far, here is a hidden "Premium" option inside the app. Use the steps below to unlock it.

  1. Open the navigation drawer from the app's Home Screen and go to Settings.
  2. Scroll to the bottom of Settings where the text "If you have any questions or suggestions" appears. Long-press this message.
    (A single tap opens the feedback screen; a long press shows a hidden counter.)
  3. When the counter appears, tap it 10 times quickly while it remains visible. If it disappears, repeat the previous step.
  4. After 10 taps the app will unlock premium features and hide ads.
  5. Completely restart the app to apply changes.
  6. Enjoy your unlocked Premium features! 😊
